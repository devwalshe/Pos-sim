The Barista Trainer

Drink Visualizer

The Drink Visualizer is an intuitive way to show drinks with their core ingredients. By interpreting the information sent by the sbuxAPI, the visualizer can generate a picture that shows exactly what percentage of the drink is sugary syrup and what part is coffee. This will help both partners and customers get a better understanding of what is going to cups.

When making these drinks, don't forget to add the love and care by hand from the heart!

Menu

The 'Menu' is a POS simulator. Using the API, partners can effectively: learn how to take actual customer orders, become familiar with the layout of the POS system, and become more comfortable asking questions to customers so they can make the moment right with every order. This project takes in many parameters to wholly simulate on-the-job scenarios, so partners feel comfortable before their first day on the floor.
